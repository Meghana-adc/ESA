Tale Trails

Branding

Tale Trails isn't just a festival, it's a vibrant community. It was born from the desire to unite readers and writers, ignite a passion for literature, and offer a platform for exploration and learning. It aspires to create a space where everyone can lose themselves in stories, be inspired by authors, and connect through their shared love of books. By offering a variety of activities, from workshops to contests to book exchanges, Tale Trails aims to be a gateway to a world of literary possibilities.
