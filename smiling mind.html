Smiling Mind

Interaction Design

The Smiling Mind app is a mindfulness and meditation platform designed to support users in cultivating mental well-being through guided meditation sessions, mindfulness exercises, and structured programs. By conducting usability tests we assessed the ease of use and user experience, and identified areas of confusion in navigation and program selection. Based on our findings we redesigned the app to improve user satisfaction and engagement.
